Our Park: The Property

There is easy access to Lake Shore for running and jogging, minutes from South Congress for shopping and a hop and a skip from South Lamar. The property has 70 condominiums and 100 townhomes. All the properties are unique and include interior atriums. Many have balconies either in the atrium or on the outside. The townhomes are 2 or 3 bedroom plans and the condominiums are 2 bedroom plans. Some of the townhomes have vaulted ceilings and all the units have wood burning fireplaces.

HOA fees cover the exterior maintenance, grounds, swimming pool and tennis courts.

How to get here

The Timber Ridge Condominiums and Town Homes are located next to downtown, 5 miles from UT and across the freeway from St. Edward's University. They are between the newly to be remodeled Riverside and Woodward streets east of I-35.
